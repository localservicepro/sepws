Our story

Founded by Ben Field, built on results

What started as a passion for turning tired, grimy surfaces into something to be proud of has grown into SEPWS — a trusted name for pressure washing, concrete sealing and external window cleaning across the region.

As an owner-operator, Ben personally handles the work and treats every property with the same care he'd give his own. There's no call-centre and no cutting corners — just a local who turns up on time, does the job properly and stands behind the finish.

From our base in Morwell we cover the Latrobe Valley and right along the South-East Melbourne corridor, helping homeowners and businesses restore their kerb appeal one driveway, wall and window at a time.
